Inspired by solid body designs from the 1970s and 1980s, the WARCHILD features a double-cut, beveled body and a 19th fret neck joint for excellent high fret access. 

Designed for riffs - big riffs - the WARCHILD speaks with a midrange heavy voice, and looks mean doing it. 

Standard features:
- 24.875" scale length
- 1 11/16” nut width
- 12” radius
- 22 frets
- Long tenon neck joint
- Two-way truss rod
- Bone nut
- Switchcraft jack and switch, CTS pots
